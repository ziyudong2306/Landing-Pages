Colorado Springs, USA - It was a bright spring morning. The air was crisp, the birds were chirping, and the sun was just rising over the horizon. I was on my way to meet Ian Janson (not his real name; name changed due to request) and could barely contain my excitement. Ian had become an overnight success in the small town of Colorado Springs and had spent the past few years guiding others.

As a motivational speaker, he has changed the lives of thousands of people. Ian was an expert at showing people their inner power and helping them overcome any obstacles they were faced with. Today, I was going to find out his secret to success and hopefully learn how to implement some of his techniques into my own life.

The secret to success

Ian invited me into his sprawling home and sat me down in the parlor. After a brief introduction, I immediately dove into a series of questions. I was on a mission to find out how he had overcome his past life's struggles and created a new one filled with fantastic opportunities.

"Well, one of my biggest struggles was a lack of focus and concentration," Ian began. "I was always full of good ideas, but I never had the energy to carry them out. Like so many people, I was stressed out, anxious, and exhausted all the time."

Ian went on to describe the typical American lifestyle. "I would wake up feeling extremely tired, rush off to work, feel frazzled the entire day, then come home, go to bed, and toss and turn all night long. Then, I would get up and do it all again the next day. I have always been a creative person, but I couldn't find the energy and drive to do anything about my ideas. I was stuck in a perpetual hamster wheel, like almost everyone else in the world. That is, until the day I decided to break free!"

Ian was obviously excited. I asked him what he did to break this vicious cycle, and he was eager to share.

"I shifted my focus onto myself for a change and made sleep my biggest priority!"

Why is sleep so important?

I have to admit hearing that sleep was the key to Ian's success was a bit surprising. I was expecting something much more profound, but Ian assured me that sleep is one of the most important things you can do to guarantee success in your life.

"When you sleep," Ian explained,"the cells of your body regenerate. You become restored and revitalized. Memories are stored in your brain. Your muscles are repaired, and your body heals itself. Without proper sleep, you wouldn't be able to survive. Unfortunately, too many people don't get enough sleep or the proper quality of sleep that they need. When this happens, they are tired, unfocused, and can't function properly. After years of poor sleeping habits, we become zombies. We push through our days and accept this as just being a normal part of life. But it isn't!"

How can a good nights sleep change your life?

I asked Ian how a good night's sleep changed his life, and he was eager to reply.

"After the very first night of having a good sleep, I immediately noticed that I had more stamina the next day. I was able to concentrate at work and found myself being much more productive. After sleeping well for a few nights in a row, I felt my mood lift. I was happier and was full of all kinds of creative ideas. I got my drive and determination back, and it was soon after this, that I decided to start my own business. It had been a dream for years, but I just didn't think I had the energy and perseverance to pull it off. Sleeping well changed all of that! Now I've been working for myself for years and sharing the secret to my success with others all across the globe!"
